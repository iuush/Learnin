The Judge in Cormac McCarthy's Blood Meridian. The character of the Judge in Blood Meridian puzzled me ever since I read it and was curious for what people here think of him. So, what the hell is he? I personally see him as a manifestation of humanity's will to dominate and enslave nature and others, and of the drive for more knowledge at all costs, in this he personifies our worst traits, propensity for violence and destruction with a complete lack of morals in places. I also like the view that he is a personification of war itself or of the United States in the eyes of the natives (tall, fat, pale, likes destroying things and knows a lot...).
